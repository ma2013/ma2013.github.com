City of Seattle Typeface Proposal




The City of Seattle is refining its branding. As part of this process, the City will hire a designer or design team to create a signature custom City of Seattle typeface for use in the official City of Seattle logo and other materials such as significant architectural signage. The City wishes to create a custom typeface in order to best achieve brand coherence that is consistent across the many departments and programs of the City, and across the many platforms, both online and physical, that exist for City communications. The City will maintain exclusive rights to the typeface.
