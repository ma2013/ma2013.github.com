Our products and bespoke consulting services are formulated to help clients better understand the opportunities and risks in the region. We provide analysis of the current newsflow and a comprehensive range of forecasts for each country in the C.I.S.

Our clients include companies already operating in the region where our forecasts and trend analysis help with budget and business planning, as well as with the preparation of regular internal and external reports. We also provide companies not yet active in the region with the analysis to better understand the business and investment climate. This includes both the opportunities as well as the risks, so that new entrants can make better informed decisions on when and how to enter the market.

Our client base includes asset managers, private wealth investors, venture capital funds and hedge funds. In addition to the monthly product and other regular ad-hoc material, we also offer a consulting service that provides portfolio managers and traders with real time analysis and opinion.

Reports will be made available to clients via email. Subscribers will also have password protected access to our online data and report library. All of our subscription services, except the Subscription Bespoke service, are payable in advance. The Subscription Bespoke service fee is separately negotiated. For asset management and hedge fund clients we can also accommodate payment through intermediaries.



Service Options:

Subscription Basic

This service offers access to our flagship monthly report, which provides analysis of events, trends and forecasts for Russia and C.I.S. countries. It provides separate commentary on macro, politics, currencies, markets, and any other topic relevant at the time of publishing. We also circulate regular intra-month ad-hoc commentaries to cover any relevant events or news stories. Subscribers also receive password protected access to our online data and report library.

This service costs $8,000 per annum.



Subscription Plus

In addition to the reports and online access available with Subscription Basic, this enhanced service offers clients the addition of email access to our analysts and one on-site visit per annum by Chris Weafer.

This service costs $12,000 per annum.



Subscription Premium

In addition to the services available under the Subscription Plus option, clients also receive 24/7 email access and may request additional notes and commentary on topics we have/are covering. Clients subscribed to this service will be offered two on-site visits by Chris Weafer per annum.

This service costs $16,000 per annum.



Subscription Bespoke

This is a fully bespoke consulting service. In addition to the regular reports and analyst access outlined in the Subscription Premium service, we can negotiate a service tailored to the specific requirements of the client. We can, for example, undertake local representative work or manage specific project(s) on behalf of the client in any C.I.S. country. For this service we can work on a retainer “plus” basis or agree a contract fee.

Note: In our cooperation with clients we regularly make presentations and attend meetings, either internally or on behalf of companies with their clients.

The fee for this service is negotiated separately with each client and based on the Subscription Premium service plus the extra work agreed.

A number of sample reports are available on our web site, in the Publications-Reports section, and may be downloaded free. We do not offer trial period subscriptions and may not respond to communications sent from non-verifiable email addresses.
